About Me

Hi! I’m Samantha, I'm a designer who codes or a developer who designs, depending on the need. I move between the empathy of user-centered design and the logic of code, creating products that not only look good but also work well — inside and out.


I’ve worked on improving product flows, collaborating with multidisciplinary teams and translating customer feedback into actionable insights.


💡 My approach: Designing interfaces that are not only beautiful but also usable. Writing clean and efficient code to bring them to life. Constantly learning and taking on new challenges in UX and development.


📌 A little about me:I started my UX journey through my experience as a Customer Excellence Analyst in a digital product environment. This role allowed me to collaborate with multidisciplinary teams and gain a deep understanding of how each area contributes to crafting better user experiences.


🚀 What drives me: Solving problems through design and technology, always with a user-centered mindset.


🔗 Want to know more about my work? Check out my projects or reach out. I’m always open to new opportunities and collaborations!
